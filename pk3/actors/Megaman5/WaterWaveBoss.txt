HARP I 0 A_JumpIfInTargetInventory("WaveHookKillFlag",1,"SuperDeathPoof")

HARP I 1 A_JumpIf(ACS_NamedExecuteWithResult("core_targetexists",1)==0,"DeathLoop1")

SuperDeathPoof:
HARP I 0 A_ChangeFlag("NOINTERACTION",1)

SuperDeath:
HARP I 0
